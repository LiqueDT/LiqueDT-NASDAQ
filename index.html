METHODOLOGY

A narrative compass, not a forecast.

LiqueDT scans recent NASDAQ-sensitive headlines for language around rates, AI/semiconductors, mega-cap earnings, volatility and risk. It assigns a small estimated directional weight, reason and confidence, then aggregates the result into bearish, balanced or bullish narrative context.

News impact is a headline estimate, not full-article verification. Cross-market effects use rolling correlation when available, but relationships can invert and price may already reflect the story. Always read the underlying source. This feature must never be used as a standalone reason to trade.
